Word Study Practice



  • You will be given a grid of 25 words and a question. The answer will be "YES" for something between 4 and 7 of the words.
  • If you'd like to instead just get practice seeing what the YES words are, try Easy Mode. In Easy Mode the answer will be YES for 24 out of the 25 options.
  • If you select the hint option in Easy Mode then the first letter of the lone exception will be below the table.
