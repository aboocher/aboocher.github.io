Blank Bingos!

This game will help you practice finding bingos that you (in theory) know and also tell you how many others there are. Upload a list of words (not alphagrams) that you know. If you don't have one, just click start and it will default to a "known" word list that is all the words containing AENRT. As you type the words, if it's a word on your list it will pop up in blue, otherwise in black.

If you get stuck, active the helpful tools. There is a hint / answer toggle. If you click on a cell, it will tell you either the first letter of all the remaining words, or just give you the answer. It is color coded as well.

There's also an animate button that will go through the whole grid and change the blank from A to Z one per second. Turn it off when you are done.
